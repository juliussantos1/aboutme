PILOT

a person who operates the flying controls of an aircraft.

What Is a Pilot? A pilot is trained to operate aircraft. As part of their duties, they file flight plans, perform maintenance checks and ensure the craft is ready for departure. This includes checking the engine, the navigation equipment and the aircraft's systems to ensure everything is running properly.
